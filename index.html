Booking a cab using XYZCabs

This topic provides information about how to book a cab using the XYZCabs application.

Overview

This procedure provides instructions to book a cab for local rides using the XYZCabs application. To book outstation rides, refer to ‘Booking a cab for outstation rides using XYZCabs’. The following table provides the estimated fares for local ride bookings:

Distance (Kms) Price (Rupees)
1-10 100
10-20 200
20-30 300
30-40 400
40-50 500

Note: The exact fare for a ride may vary depending on the availability of cabs and the time of booking.

Prerequisites

Make sure that you have the following:

  • XYZCabs installed
  • A valid user account
  • An active internet connection

Procedure

Sign in to XYZCabs

Complete the following steps to sign in to XYZCabs:

  1. Open the XYZCabs application.
  2. Click Sign In to access to your account.
  3. Enter your Username and Password and click Ok.
    The login procedure is complete and the XYZCabs homepage displays.

Book a new ride

Complete the following steps to book a new ride:

  1. Click the New Ride icon on the top-right corner of the homepage.
  2. In the New Ride dialog box, enter your Pickup and Drop locations.
  3. Select the type of cab you need from the Cab Type drop-down menu. The available options are:
  • 3 Seater
  • 5 Seater
  • 7 Seater
  1. Click Book.
  2. In the Ride Review dialog box, review the arrival time and the fare and click Confirm Ride to book a ride.

The application searches for a nearby cab and contacts the driver. This process takes less than a minute and you will receive a notification with cab details when a ride is confirmed.
